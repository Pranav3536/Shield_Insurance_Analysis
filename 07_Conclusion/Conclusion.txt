The Shield Insurance Analysis project successfully transformed raw insurance datasets into meaningful business insights using 
Power BI, SQL and Python.

The dashboard provided a clear understanding of customer demographics, revenue performance, sales trends and policy analysis across 
different cities and sales channels.

The analysis identified key business drivers such as high-performing cities, major customer age groups and effective sales modes 
contributing to company growth.

Overall, this project demonstrates how data analytics and interactive dashboards can support better business decision-making and improve 
overall operational performance.
