The Shield Insurance dashboard was designed using key business KPIs to monitor company performance effectively.

Main KPIs included:
- Total Revenue Generated
- Total Customers
- Daily Revenue Growth
- Daily Customer Growth
- Revenue by Sales Mode
- Customer Distribution by Age Group
- City-wise Revenue Performance
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Policy-wise Premium Collection
- Settlement Percentage Analysis

These KPIs provided a clear overview of business growth, customer trends, and operational performance.
