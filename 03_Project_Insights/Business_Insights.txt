The analysis revealed several important business insights for Shield Insurance.

- Delhi NCR generated the highest revenue and customer count among all cities.
- Customers aged between 31–40 years contributed the maximum premium revenue.
- Offline-Agent sales mode performed better than other sales channels in terms of revenue generation.
- Online platforms showed steady customer growth, indicating increasing digital adoption.
- Certain insurance policies consistently generated higher premium collections.
- Monthly trend analysis identified peak business periods with strong customer acquisitions and revenue growth.

These insights helped in understanding customer behavior, market performance, and overall business growth patterns.
